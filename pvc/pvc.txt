1. The Relationship Hierarchy
Think of it like a chain. Breaking the first link doesn't destroy the others.

[Pod] ---uses---> [PVC] ---bound to---> [PV] ---points to---> [Actual Data]

Delete the Pod: The PVC and PV remain Bound. The data is safe.

Delete the PVC: This is where things change. What happens to the PV depends on the Reclaim Policy.
