Dynamic Provisioning.

Because you used the storageClassName: local-path (which is the default "factory" that comes with Rancher Desktop), you no longer have to manually create the Persistent Volume (PV) yourself.

How Dynamic Provisioning Works (The Magic)
The PVC Request: You tell Kubernetes, "I need 2Gi of space, and I want to use the local-path worker."

The Provisioner: The local-path controller (a background process in Rancher Desktop) sees your request.

The PV Creation: The controller automatically creates a brand new PV for you. It picks a folder inside the Rancher VM and labels it as yours.

The Binding: It instantly "staples" (binds) that new PV to your PVC.
