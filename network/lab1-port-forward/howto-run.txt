Step 3: Port-Forward to the Service

kubectl get svc

$ kubectl get svc
NAME          TYPE        CLUSTER-IP     EXTERNAL-IP   PORT(S)   AGE
kubernetes    ClusterIP   10.43.0.1      <none>        443/TCP   92m
web-service   ClusterIP   10.43.121.53   <none>        80/TCP    8m51s
$

kubectl port-forward svc/web-service 9000:80

curl localhost:9000
